# Queue Triage

Purpose: periodically re-align queue scope with current product priorities.

Use this as an eval-system procedure, not a project-template file.

## Steps

1. Remove items that are already complete/obsolete.
2. Refuse or archive items that are off-center for current focus.
3. Re-scope oversized items into smaller project docs.
4. Ensure `queue.md`, `active/`, and `backlog/` are consistent.
